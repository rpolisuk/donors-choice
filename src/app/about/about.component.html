History


           According to a 2017 CBC News report, “$173 million in worn or used clothing was exported from Canada to countries and regions around the world”. Diabetes Canada working with Value Village raises $8.7 million annually through its clothing donation program. As well as the Kidney Foundation of Canada working with Value Village has a clothing donation program.

All the charities in Canada have their own websites and apps which makes donation inconvenient. If a person wants to donate something, then he/she would have to go through all the websites and find a suitable website that accepts such donations and at the end after spending lots to time they are finding such organization he/she would eventually drop or postpone the plan.

This project is to a minimum viable product (MVP) of the Donor’s Choice web site which will allow Canadians to donate their gently used clothing to the charity of their choice and to schedule the pickup from a central web site.
