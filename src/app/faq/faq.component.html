Acceptable Donations

At Donors Choice, we treat every “guest” or resident with all the respect. We treat them the way we want to be treated ourselves. That means we never give someone an item of clothing or pair of shoes that we would not be willing to wear ourselves. We need quality clothing and shoes of all sizes for men, women, and children.

We ask that all donations of used clothing be clean, of good quality, and ready to wear (no repairs needed). Please sort, box, and label all clothes according to gender (men, women, or children) and mark the box as “used.”
